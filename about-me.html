About Us

We at the NUS Student Chapter of the ACM strives towards our primary objective, learning. We actively organizes the most interesting events, talks and workshops for the technologically inclined. We also aim to create an environment with numerous opportunities to learn and apply programming skills by undertaking real life projects. We are looking forward to contributing positively to the society by taking up projects which require technical expertise from NGOs and social enterprises as well as projects that can help the NUS student community.
