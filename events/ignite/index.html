Ignite is an exhilirating style of presentation where the speaker is given only 5 minutes to convey their passion to the world. The speaker is given 20 slides with 5 seconds for each slide and must then carry his presentation in an almost story-telling like manner, relying a great deal on improvisation and presence. But above all he must cpature the audiences attention in the few words he says. The ignition of fire of creativity among the crowd is the essence of the whole speech. That is the whole challenge and spirit of Ignite. Think you are up for it?
